Park Cleanup


About

As part of the Active Citizenship Project, my class decided to initiate a park clean up at Tiong Bahru to conserve the cleanliness of the environment.


The Learning Process

  • Unexpectedly, we were met with multiple types of trash at the park, even though Singapore has been labelled as a clean country
  • Through this, it instill a level of responsibility in me to continue upkeeping Singapore's environment so as to stay true to its name.
  • Afterwards, we created an Instagram post and presentation to advocate for this cause.
