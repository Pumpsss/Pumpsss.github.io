About

During my time in the Integrated Diploma Program (IDP), I underwent several design camps to hone my problem-solving and pitching skills. There are 3 main camps highlighted in IDP that is: ARTC Camp, SUTD-Big D Camp & Hackathon. Through this, I will be more prepared to becoming a technopreneur in the future.

My Learning Process

  • I learnt how to work in teams consisting of people with different Applied Subject (AS) background.
  • I am became more equipped with ways to approach a problem and to develop a product.
  • I learnt how to properly deliver a pitch and construct a solid market plan.
