2024 GMAC Student's Challenge


About

Participants are tasked to research and create an infographic based on the theme "What is CRISPR-Cas9 Technology and its Applications?". Through which, it promotes active learning and stimulates student's interest in science based GM-related topics.


My Learning Process

Week 1: Discussion of chosen topics with group members
Week 2: Research of chosen topic and crafting of infographic
Week 3: Feedback from teachers and refining of infographic


My Key Takeaways

This project has been extremely enriching as it allowed me to further understand what is genetically modified organisms (GMOs) and more importantly, their applications in the medical and economic fields, potentially mitigating global challenges such as poverty. This experience allowed me the opportunity to apply my prior knowledge in Gene Technology and GMOs beyond the classroom, probing me to delve more into the conflicts brought up by GMOs such as the public's concerns pertaining it and its ethical considerations.
