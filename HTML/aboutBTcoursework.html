IDP Biotech Coursework


About

As part of IDP's integrated learning, students are tasked to independantly conduct an experiment on proteins through forming a step-by-step procedure, variables, hyphotesis and lab report. Through which, we are able to integrate our prior knowledge in Biotechnology, more specificall Gene Technology, to tackle real world problems. This project also provides us the freedom to explore beyond syllabus and think of innovative ways to conduct an experiment.


The Learning Process


Week 1: Brainstorm ideas and form experimental outlines to be vetted by teachers


Week 2-4: Conduct experiments in lab for 3h per week


Week 5-6: Writing of reports and submission of project
