Ramadan On Wheels


About

During the Ramadan month of 2022, I led a food drive as chairperson to arrange food to be distributed to under-priviledged families and uplift the spirit of Ramadan in them.


The Learning Process

  • First, we bought a bunch of kuihs and ketupats to be given to the families
  • Next, we set up a booth for teachers and adults to donate their money for these food to be sent off
  • Lastly, we gave a presentation to remind students to bring any food they may want to donate to these families such as pasta and pasta sauce.
  • We ensured that all food donated are halal and have a long expiration date left.
