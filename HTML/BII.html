Internship at BII



About

As part of the IDP Structured Work and Research Attachment Program, I was offered a 1 week internship at the Bioinformatics Institute of Singapore, (BII).


The Task


We had to conduct brief interviews with the various researchers on their views on the use of AI such as ChatGPT in their works


The Learning Process

  • Once we reached the office, we had a tour and familiarised ourselves with the researchers there.
  • Afterwards, we curated specialised questions to interview each role in BII such as interns, research fellow, etc.
  • We had an enjoyable moment and even went out to lunch with them.
  • Click here to read one of their feedbacks



Key Takeaways

  • Overall, I was able to witness the working environment of researchers,
  • Learnt how computing and biotechnology are integral in research,
  • Build up confidence in interviewing skills and
  • Able to meet and communicate with passionate researchers
