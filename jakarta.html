BBS x SST Jakarta Trip


About

As a continuition of the Chemistry iPT, we are tasked to measure and compare the air quality of Singapore and Indonesia with students from Sekolah Bina Bangsa (BBS). Through which, we also spent one week in Jakarta and got to understand more of its culture.


The Learning Process

  • We visited BBS and conducted the experiment.
  • Afterwards, our buddy showed us around the school and we accompanied them during classes
  • Lastly, we had a short tour of the historical places in Indonesia such as an Indonesian hamlet.
