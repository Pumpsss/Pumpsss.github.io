A*STAR Research Exposure Programme


About

Awardees will have the opportunity to attend a 3-day Research Exposure Programme where they will visit A*STAR research institutes, interact with A*STAR researchers and participate in a research workshop. Awardees will be invited to A*STAR seminars, outreach events and programmes, as well as talks by A*STAR scientists. Awardees will receive periodic updates on the latest research work at A*STAR and happenings in science and research.
