2024 GMAC Student's Challenge


About

We are tasked to conduct an experiment to measure the air quality at SST using MQ135 air sensor and an arduino kit. Through which, we are to produce a presentation to pitch for a change in SST to reduce the CO2 levels in the atmosphere.


The Learning Process

  • First, we brainstormed the research question that is, "How does the amount of carbon dioxide in the air, near the drop-off point in SST, affect the learning progress of students aged 13-16 through the use of components such as the MQ13?"
  • Afterwards, we set the MQ135 sensor at the SST drop off area in the morning, afternoon and evening to obtain 3 different sets of data.
  • Finally, using those data, we pitched possible changes to SST and created an interactive inforgraphic along side to advocate for these changes.
