Games4Migrants


About

As part of the Active Citizenship Education project, I was tasked to create a suitable game to familiarise foreign workers and migrants to Singapores cuisines


The Learning Process

  • After much brainstormign and feedback from the teachers, I decided to develop a game similar to SNAP, but with the different food cuisines instead.
  • Through that, I gained the importance of appreciating forgein talents in Singapore and to welcome them more openly.
